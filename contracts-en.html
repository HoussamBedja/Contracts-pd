Proactive Disclosure of Contracting Visualization Tool


In an effort to maximize transparency and accessibility to the Canadian public, the Procurement Policy Division alongside the Open Government Division in the Treasury Board Secretariat has created a visualization tool that reflects the statistics from the under and over $10K databases for the proactive disclosure of contracts.

As outlined in the Guidelines on Proactive Disclosure of Contracts, departments are required to publically disclose contracts above $10K quarterly as well as publically disclose aggregated totals of contracts below $10K annually. For further information on the proactive disclosure of contracts, please consult the Open Government Contracts page to access both databases as well as the Guidelines on Proactive Disclosure of Contracts for further information on the type of data disclosed.

The interactive visualization tool allows the user to configure the data to obtain the most useful and relevant data for their needs. It offers easy and intuitive access to data, as well as the ability to compare information and select slices specific for organizations.

The visualization tool is updated daily to maximize its’ accuracy for the user.
