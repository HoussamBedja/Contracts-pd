Procurement Contracts Report


Possible delays in proactive publication of information

Openness, transparency and accountability are guiding principles of the Government of Canada. However, the Government of Canada's ability to meet timelines for proactive publication may be affected by the current circumstances and exceptional measures put in place to curb the spread of the novel coronavirus (COVID-19) and protect the health and safety of our employees. As a result there may be delays in the proactive publication of information during this period. Making government information available to Canadians proactively continues to be important to us. We will continue to make reasonable efforts to meet the timelines for the proactive disclosure of government information, in accordance with operational realities. Thank you in advance for your patience and understanding during this period as we all navigate these unprecedented challenges.

Introduction

Openness, transparency and accountability are guiding principles of the Government of Canada.

The contract information displayed in the following tables and charts is a consolidation of contract data provided by government institutions under the Access to Information Act and pursuant to the Guidelines on the Proactive Disclosure of Contracts. These reports reflect the government commitment to publicly disclose contract information with only very limited exceptions, such as national security.

This interactive report allows readers to filter the tables and charts to obtain the most useful and relevant data for their needs. It offers easy and intuitive access to data, as well as the ability to compare information and select specific organizations.

Table Summary

The tables and reports will be updated as proactive publication of contract data from the most recently available calendar year are reported by government institutions.

Numbers in the following tables may not add up due to rounding. All dollar amounts indicated are in Canadian dollars and includes all applicable fees and taxes.


Total number of original contracts awarded and total value of contracts and net amendments awarded, (Contracts above $10,000; Contracts $10,000 and under, or both) – by contract type




Contracts above $10,000: Total number of original contracts awarded and total value of contracts and net amendments awarded – by solicitation procedure



Notes to the Procurement Contracts Report:

1. Objective

The objective of this Procurement Contracts Report is to provide Canadians with information on the Government of Canada’s annual procurement contracts statistics.

It is important to note that there is no statutory requirement for the Procurement Contracts Report. This report summarizes contract information already disclosed in the Open Government Portal. For more information on the proactive disclosure of contracts, please consult the Open Government Contracts page to access the quarterly and annual reporting databases as well as the Guidelines on Proactive Disclosure of Contracts for further information on the type of data disclosed in this report.

The information contained in these reporting tables has been derived from unaudited data provided by the authorized federal reporting entities (see section 2). As such, no assurances can be provided as to the accuracy, completeness, currency or reliability of the data, and the Government of Canada does not offer any guarantee in this regard.

For information on the Government of Canada’s purchasing activities prior to calendar year 2017, refer to the annual Purchasing Activity Reports.

2. Reporting entities

The data in this report are from federal entities defined as a government entity in s.81 of the Access to Information Act.

3. Contract amendments

The data in this report includes contract amendments as a net value (e.g. total value of all positive value and negative value amendments combined).

4. Acquisition cards

The data in this report does not include transactions made through acquisition cards.

5. Other excluded items

The data in this report do not include expenditures for travel, hotel accommodation, hospitality and petty cash.
